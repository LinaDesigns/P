VESSiL VESSiL is a medical app proposed by the Cardiology Department at UI Health Hospital. Its purpose is to serve as a platform for patients to store their personal stent surgery information. This reliable application would provide patients with quick access to knowledge about stent surgery and post operative care. As a simple and user friendly tool, VESSiL aims to be accessible for all users in any environment.

Subdog Many dog owners have experienced the struggle of not being able to keep their furry friend still while giving them a bath. Subdog is a product in the making with the goal of providing dog owners an easier dog-washing experience. The aim is to create a ridged feeding toy that suctions to the wall. In doing so, pets are distracted by treats allowing the owner for a more comfortable washing.

Piezoboys Piezoboys is a start-up brand focused on creating an eco-friendly and energy-efficient piezoelectric, electricity caused by pressure, tires. The bike consists of tires that have piezos embedded around, with the goal of generating electricity while pedaling without adding resistance to the biker.

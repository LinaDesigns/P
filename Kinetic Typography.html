Kinetic Typography

Kinetic Typography is "moving text.” It is an animation technique that mixes motion and text in order to express ideas through video animation. Shown on this page is a collection of Kinetic Typography exercises.

The first video is a remake of the title sequence for the movie, “There’s Something About Mary.” The challenge is to depict the essence and vibe of the movie using typographic and form elements in harmony. Shown next are exercises using the word “flow” and how the fluidity of the term flow can be depicted through Kinetic Typography.

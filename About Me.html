About Me

We live in a world consumed by a desire for constant advancements and blinded by the next new trend. But the desire for continued improvements of everything from technology to products to environments, in addition to societal pressures to conform, distorts our impression of ourselves and, despite every newfangled thing, makes us very unhappy. The norm says: look this way, talk this way, dress this way, be this way, act this way. The norm repudiates difference. No one can meet its toxic standards. As a Korean American female designer with a secondary passion in the field of Communcation, I am especially interested in overturning the norm and celebrating difference. As a firm believer that design and communication work hand in hand, irony is my communication strategy. Through the exploration of thoughtful detail and individual expression, I strive to bring new experiences and efficiencies to people's lives. I hope to aid in showing society that design is not just a form of Art, but that is a way of life!

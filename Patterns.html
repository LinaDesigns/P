Patterns and Textures

Pattern is a fundamental design principle. It is a specific type of visual texture derived from repeated structures approving in an organized manner. It has a strong relationship to Geometry.

Texture is the visual or tactile surface characteristics and appearance of something. The illusion of a physical texture is a visual texture. An implied texture is a visual texture which is usually an abstraction. Tactile texture is physical, it can be touched.

Shown in this collection are the various creations of the patterns and textiles made over time.

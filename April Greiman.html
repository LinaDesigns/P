April Greiman on Technology

April Greiman is known as a major influential figure within the design community. Being one of the first designers to adopt computer technology as a tool for graphic design, she has been one of the founders in structuring the New Wave design style to the United States. Greiman’s ability to combine analog and digital platforms has and continues to inspire many of the design trends we see arise today.

Created using a grid layout system, this projects includes Greiman’s discussion and interview on technology with Josh Smith. Intertwining Greiman’s artwork with her philosophy on design, this book is an ode to her continued legacy as a figure in the design field.
